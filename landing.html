Your Dedicated Family Doctor Awaits

At KNUST, we're committed to providing you and your family with personalized healthcare. With the Family Practice Model, you’ll have a dedicated family doctor who understands your unique needs and medical history. Enjoy consistent, tailored care and streamlined appointments that make managing your health simpler and more effective.

Why the Family Practice Model?

Discover the advantages of having a dedicated family doctor for you and your loved ones.

1

Personalized Care:

Receive care tailored to your unique medical history and needs. Your dedicated family doctor will have a comprehensive understanding of your health, allowing for more personalized and effective treatment.

2

Consistent Healthcare Provider:

Enjoy continuity with a familiar doctor for all your appointments. Build a lasting relationship with your family doctor, reducing the need to repeatedly explain your medical history and ensuring consistent care.

3

Streamlined Appointments:

Simplify your healthcare experience with easier appointment scheduling. Access your family doctor’s schedule and book appointments with ease, minimizing wait times and improving convenience.

4

Enhanced Satisfaction:

Benefit from improved overall satisfaction with your healthcare services. The Family Practice Model aims to enhance your overall experience by providing a more personalized and attentive approach to healthcare.

How the Family Practice Model Works

Follow these simple steps to get started with personalized and continuous healthcare.

1 Sign Up: register your interest by filling out the online form. Provide basic details about yourself and your family to get started.

2 Doctor Assignment: register your interest by filling out the online form. Choose or be assigned a dedicated family doctor. Our system will match you with a doctor based on your needs or preferences.

3 Schedule Appointments: register your interest by filling out the online form. View your doctor’s availability and book appointments. Manage your healthcare easily with a user-friendly scheduling system.

4 Ongoing Care:Receive continuous and personalized care from your family doctor. Your doctor will be familiar with your medical history and provide tailored treatment.

The Family Practice Model is available exclusively to KNUST university staff and their dependents. If you are a staff member, you can enroll and benefit from this model.
